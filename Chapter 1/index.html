Taco time: Your Taco Connection

Breakfast Tacos are a great way to start the day, no matter what time it is.

A great Breakfast Taco, piccture from Pexels

Recpie and serving instructions as follows:

Ingredients

  • 300g Flour
  • 6g salt
  • 2g baking powder
  • 3/4C Warm Water
  • 1/4C Oil(vegetable is best)
  • 6 large egs
  • 6 peices of bacon
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Chives
  • Cilantro Lime salsa

Method

  1. Combine Dry Ingredients in the bowl of a stand mixer
  2. Add Wet Ingredients
  3. Mix on low until smooth and combined
  4. Roll into balls
  5. Flatten balls into tortilla shape
  6. Toast in cast iron skillet on med high until done
  7. Add scrambeled eggs
  8. Add Bacon
  9. Add Chives and Lime salse
  10. Enjoy!
